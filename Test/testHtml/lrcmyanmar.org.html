Pomelo for Myanmar is Crowdfunding!

At Pomelo they have proven that great handcrafted design support by Fair Trade principles can provide livelihoods for many of the poorest members of our communities. They are aiming to raise $25,000 in 25 days for internal and international expansion. Their aim is to reach more artisan groups throughout Myanmar and to be able to export some of their products to a more global audience.
